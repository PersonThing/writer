Published in Outlook Magazine 2022 in New Delhi, and in Abobo Zine 2023 in New York, this is a growing section of the different works to come. You can also find them directly on my Substack

## On Writing

The pen and the screen are fighting, which is the superior mode of writing? Which camp are you in? A poem that'll have you changing your mind, and changing sides.

## Hannah Banana

We give you our hearts when we have nothing else. Why do we value it so little? Why don't we give it when we have everything else? This is a birthday poem for Hannah, a heart for a heart

## My Dad, Socks & Gulzar

A writer's father's day wish to their interfering father.

## Medea's Ideas

Abortion rights will make sure we don't resort to what Medea did - kill her living sons.

## Home & Hell

A poem on the homes we live in, what the walls have seen, how fast they deteriorate and how hopeful we were when we first got there

## Times Square

A poem to be read slowly with lots of pregnant pauses. A rash caused by the shining lights coming off the billboards
