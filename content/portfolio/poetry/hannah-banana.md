Hannah
Banana
It’s gna
get worse
with age and time
what wrinkles may come
in the head and the heart
as friends and lovers
do soon depart
to make room for another
bunch of brothers and sisters.

Hannah banana
It’s gna get better
because people who stay
would never settle
for anything less
than the heart of a poet
and the haircut of Elvis.
O’Hara quoting goddess
oh please tell me this:

Where did you learn to love like that?
To wear your heart on your sleeve
And your pain as a mild peeve

How much space is there
that it fits 80 people on Halloween
and one Hamlet dissing queen.

Your name is a mirror.
In my language it says inverted things
where ‘Han’ means yes
And ‘nah’ means no
and right down the middle
is perhaps an inverted being

with a booming laugh
and the politest shade.
Didn’t Kant say
‘No Straight Thing Was Ever Made’?

All sharp edges in nature
come from being broken
It takes time to be
rounded into gentleness

So take time dear one
35, 47 and hopefully soon 51
Let changes shape you every year
Every day a softer day
for my darling - happy birthday !
