## Brief

This was part of an in-house custom content created for the brand. Part of a larger content strategy deployed across FB, Insta, Youtube and Tiktok, and a page launch on site.

Since the woodworking family featured in the film withdrew permissions to feature their name, we made a hard pivot from focusing on the ‘slow life’ ethos of Grand Seiko to the ‘handmade’ ethos.
