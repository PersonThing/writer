# Amazon Designer Boutique

- Showcase the width of collections across different product lines (saris, lehengas, shararas)
- Menswear covers 13% of this shopping category, represent accordingly.
- Mood board to be ‘formal/ elevated’ achieved through a minimal, dark background.
- Use of Indian models prioritised.
