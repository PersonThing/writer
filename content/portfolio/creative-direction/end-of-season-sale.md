The merchandising experience of the online store focused on creating multiple sale opportunities with navigation on <5000rs, <1000rs, <500rs to cater to sale and bargain hunters. Visual Language focuses on colour pops on greyscale, exuberant body language to cater to a younger, tech savvy and price conscious target audience.
