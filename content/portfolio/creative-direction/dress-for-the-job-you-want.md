To stimulate buying at the start of the financial year (March 2019) and internship/ hiring season we focus on separates that can be mixed-and-matched to create a capsule wardrobe ideal for interviewing, under three different tiers of budget.

This was exclusively catered for menswear to pump performance that was not on track for its YoY predictions.
