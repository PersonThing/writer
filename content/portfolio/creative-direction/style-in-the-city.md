Concept based shoot Style in the City which relies on the famous TV show characters and its strong style language to gamify shopping here as a buzzfeed style “Which fashionista are you?” and surface maximum trends:

- Layering
- Ankle Boots
- Accordion pleats
- Sheer tops
- Americana
- Chunky Knits
